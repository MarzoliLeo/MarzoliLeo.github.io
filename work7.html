007 : Block Breaker

This was one of my first game developed and I was inspired by the old cool videogames kinda retro. I wanted to experiment and I tried to develop this game making it as much fun as I coult at the time. In this game you move a paddle in order to hit a ball which bounce and if it a block you break it, but some of them are special and make some effects. You win if you pass every level.

This game is offline, for computer and WebGL. Developed in 6 days.
